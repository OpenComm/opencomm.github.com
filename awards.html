Awards

OpenComm is an award-winning team with a great group dynamic and support from several faculty members. Our work on sound spacialization is a particular point of interest and is one of the aspects that make OpenComm a unique tool for video conferences.

"BOOM is an annual showcase of student research and creativity in digital technology and applications. It's an opportunity for Cornell undergraduate and Master's-level students to show their best work in cutting-edge technologies to the public and the Cornell community. BOOM is free and open to the public. All visitors can participate in demonstrations and vote on their favorite project, and there will be refreshments and door prizes." - Cornell BOOM 2011 Last year. OpenComm received the Cisco Pioneer Awards and GE Imagination in IT Award.
