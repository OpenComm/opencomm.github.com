Join Us

Apply now! OpenComm is an award-winning team with a great group dynamic and support from several faculty members.

Advantages

Our team allows students to explore the following areas:
Management experience
Working with professor Graeme Bailey
Rapid software development
Experience with a small team of both graduate and undergraduate students
Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)
Design and Security (HCISec)
Taking research for 1-4 credits (MEng Project applicable)
Designing concepts for real time communication

What will I be doing?

Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) Design:
Design a user experience that improves the current online conference experience
Human-Computer Interaction Security (HCISec):
Design and implement a mobile application security system
Software Development:
Implement the software designed by the Design team
Publication:
Design and implement the team website; Manage creation of publication materials (research papers, user manuals, posters, presentation demos)

How do I get involved?

We welcome undergraduates and graduates from all majors and all levels of experience. Contact the project leader Risa Naka at CUOpenComm@gmail.com to schedule an interview by September 12 (Mon). You can also apply through our site.
