Interested in joining our team

Come research for credit with OpenComm, an award-winning Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Security (HCISec) group working with Professor Graeme Bailey to develop new design and security concepts for real time communication.

If you're interested in:

  • Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) Design and Security (HCISec)
  • Taking research for 1-4 credits (MEng Project applicable)
  • Designing concepts for real time communication.
  • Management experience
  • Working with Professor Graeme Bailey
  • Rapid software development
  • Experience with a team of both graduate and undergraduate students

We welcome Cornell undergraduate and graduate students from all majors and all levels of experience. Contact our project leader Risa Naka at cuopencomm@gmail.com to schedule an interview.
