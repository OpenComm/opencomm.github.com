Sound Spacialization

Sound Spacialization allows you to virtually manipulate the conference space on your phone. As you move the participants of the call, you create a 2D audio effect. Some will appear louder as you move them closer and others quieter. Unlike conventional video conferencing applications, how you experience the conference is dictated entirely by you.

  • Side Chats

    Ever had a quick thought you wanted to whisper to someone in the conference? With side chats, you can now have private conversations within the conference. Use it to privately discuss about the conference, plan ahead what to say with a partner, or just for fun. Prioritize your conversations and take your conference call to a new level.

  • Accessibility

    OpenComm simplifies setting up the conference, navigating between side chats, and changing the conference settings. Not only will you have complete and unrestricted control over the conference but also everything will be laid out and visible from the start.
